The flooring solution for our later life people

PROBLEM

In later life people deal with many different ailments:

  • incontinence
  • alzheimer
  • dementia
  • anxiety
  • etc...

Some of those illnesses may require immediate preventative action and others may simply need intermittent or continuous monitoring.

We know this can be difficult; monitoring a later lifer 24hrs a day, can be very expensive for families and the care industry.

SOLUTION

Our aim is to create an unintrusive, people centered monitoring system that maximizes the care eco-system using gait tracking to:

  • monitor
  • learn behaviours
  • identify patterns
  • predict
  • counteract

ailment that may impact a later lifers living independently.



BUSINESS STRATEGY

MeAround could be used in the following scenarios:

  • later lifer & relatives
  • later lifer & doctor
  • later lifer & nurse care
  • Planning and development of more people-centered care facilities based on path data gathered with meAround

We plan to become market leaders by pursuing future innovation opportunities, because we know that by 2030 there will 19 million later lifers over the age of 65 in the UK. It is projected 6.2 million of those will have a long term limiting illness or disability. *

www.ageuk.org.uk

TECHNOLOGY

Infi-Tex quantum tunneling composite is a pressure switching textile Sensor that will send raw pressure data to the cloud, allowing for real time gait monitoring.

IBM-Watson will be the artificial intelligence (AI) and analytical software that will receive the data and allow us to learn behaviours, identify patterns and predict

Mobile appUsed to counteract the actions to take. Notifications, changes in patterns, communication
